Failstacking Guide
===================

Use cheap accessories to get to **2** failstacks. Use PRI accessories acquired from before to get to 5 failstacks. Use reblath or any cheap armor to get to the desired number of failstacks. All calculations are based on the cost of memory fragments, armor stones, and weapon stones.

The following amounts were determined by 
$$
(Blackstone\, Cost)\times \frac{1}{P(success)}+(Memory\, Fragent\, Cost)\times P(failure)+(Failstacks\, Cost)
$$
